Welcome to Hoodie!


We are a community of many different people. Some of us are developers, others are non-coding experts. Some of us love coffee, others prefer tea, some of us like cheesecake, others prefer Burritos. All of us enjoy a good night’s sleep, and all of us believe in an independent, decentralised, diverse future of the internet, and we want Hoodie to do its part to turn this into reality. This is why we’re working on this Open Source project. Together, we are strong. Together, we are the Hoodie Community.
