Say hi to the Hoodies!

We are a community of many different people, amongst us are developers as well as non-coding experts. What we have in common is that we all enjoy a good night’s sleep, and we all believe in working on an independent, decentralised future of the internet in diverse communities, and we want Hoodie to do its part to turn this into reality. This is why we’re working on this Open Source project. Together, we are strong. Together, we are the Hoodie Community.
